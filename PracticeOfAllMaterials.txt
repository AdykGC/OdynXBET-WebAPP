Exercise 1:
  1) Create a table called Products with the following columns:
    |ProductID (integer, primary key)
    |ProductName (varchar)
    |Category (varchar)
    |Price (varchar)
  2) Add a new column Country (varchar) to the Products table
  3) Insert one product into the Products table:
    |ProductID: 1, ProductName: 'Laptop', Category: 'Electronics', Price: 899.99, Country: 'USA'
  4) Insert four more products into the Products table:
    |ProductID: 2, ProductName: 'Book', Category: 'Books', Price: 19.99, Country: 'USA' 
    |ProductID: 3, ProductName: 'T-shirt', Category: 'Apparel', Price: 14.99, Country: 'Turkey' 
    |ProductID: 4, ProductName: TV, Category: Electronics, Price: 699.99, Country: 'Japan' ProductID: 5, 
    |ProductName: 'Dress', Category: 'Apparel', Price:99.99, Country: 'China'
  5) Update the price of the 'Laptop' to 999.99 in the Products table
  6) Retrieve all products in the 'Electronics' category from the Products table.
  7) Delete the product with the price < 20from the Products table
  8) Remove all rows from the Products table
  9) Delete Products table
